01 — About

Biography

From 1981 to 1997, Dr. R. W. R. Darling held various academic positions, culminating as a Full Professor at the University of South Florida, Tampa. He then joined the National Security Agency in Maryland, serving in the Mathematics Research Group from 1998 to 2026, most recently within the Statistics and Machine Learning Division.

His external publications focus on scalable graph algorithms, stochastic models of large spatio-temporal networks, and categorical frameworks for access control in multi-agent systems.
